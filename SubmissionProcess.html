Submission Process

  • Manuscript Preparation: Manuscripts should be written in English and formatted according to the journal's style guide. The text must be double-spaced, using a standard font (e.g., Times New Roman, 12-point size). Figures and tables should be included in the main document, each with a descriptive caption.
  • Cover Letter: Include a cover letter with the submission, summarizing the main findings and significance of the work.
  • Author Information: Provide full names, affiliations, and contact information for all authors. Indicate the corresponding author who will handle correspondence regarding the manuscript.
  • Submission Method:All manuscripts files must sended via Emial of editorial board with title of Mnacript on header zain0011@aun.edu.eg
