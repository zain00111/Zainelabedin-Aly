Welcome to the Journal of Applied Physics and Engineering

The National Journal of Applied Physics aims to provide a platform for researchers and practitioners in the field of applied physics to share their discoveries and innovations. We publish peer-reviewed articles covering a wide range of topics including, but not limited to:

  • Quantum Mechanics
  • Optical Physics
  • Material Science
  • Nanotechnology
  • Semiconductor Physics
  • Photonic Devices
  • Thermodynamics

Our mission is to advance the knowledge and understanding of applied physics through rigorous research and scientific dialogue. We encourage submissions from both established and early-career researchers, fostering an inclusive academic environment.
